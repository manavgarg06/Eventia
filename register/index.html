F.A.Q

  • How can I access the online event platform?

    Once you have registered for the event, you will receive an email with instructions and a unique link to access the online event platform.

  • What equipment or software do I need to join the online event?

    To join the online event, you will need a stable internet connection and a device such as a computer, laptop, or smartphone. Additionally, we recommend using the latest version of a web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari

  • Can I participate in the event from anywhere in the world?

    Absolutely! Our online event is accessible globally, so you can participate from anywhere with an internet connection.

  • Can I ask questions or interact with the presenters during the event?

    Yes, there will be interactive sessions and opportunities to ask questions during the event. Details on how to participate and engage will be provided closer to the event date.

  • Will there be any technical support available during the event?

    Yes, we will have a dedicated technical support team available throughout the event. They can assist you with any technical issues or questions you may have.
